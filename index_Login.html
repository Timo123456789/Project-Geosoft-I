Corona Warn App "Lite"

course: Geosoftware I SoSe 2020
authors: Timo Lietmeyer & Judith Bresser

Protect yourself and others

The use of public transport increases the risk of transmission of infectious diseases.
The Corona Warn App "Lite" was developed to protect yourself and your fellow human beings.

The webapp allows users to save which ones they have taken, so that they are notified when one of these trips has been classified as a risk of infection.

Log in!

First create a new user with a username and password.
You can define your role with the corresponding button.
Please create all users first before you log in.

After using the app, you can log out with the logout function.


Take part - for you, your family and friends

Never give your password to others.
